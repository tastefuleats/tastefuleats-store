Holiday-Perfect Honey Baked Ham

This isn’t just a ham—it’s a ritual. Inspired by the iconic Honey Baked Ham, this recipe brings the caramelized crust, the tender spiral slices, and the emotional warmth of holiday gatherings into your kitchen. From TastefulEats.store, where every bite is a vessel.

Ingredients

  • 1 spiral-sliced bone-in ham (8–10 lbs)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Place ham in roasting pan, cut side down.
  2. Mix brown sugar, honey, mustard, cloves, and cinnamon into a glaze.
  3. Brush glaze over ham. Cover loosely with foil and bake for 1.5 hours.
  4. Uncover, brush again, and bake another 30 minutes until caramelized.
  5. Let rest 10 minutes. Slice and serve with memory.
