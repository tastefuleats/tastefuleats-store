Soup for Silence

A grief recipe for quiet evenings. This soup carries memory, warmth, and ritual.

There are nights when the silence feels heavier than the air. This soup was born from one of those nights—when grief needed warmth, not words. It’s simple, soft, and slow. A ritual in a bowl.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 leek, sliced thin
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 small potato, peeled and cubed
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Warm the oil in a quiet pan. Let the leek soften slowly.
  2. Add broth and potato. Simmer gently for 20 minutes.
  3. Blend if you wish. Serve warm, with silence.
