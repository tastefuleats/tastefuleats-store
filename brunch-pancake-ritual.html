Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes with Berry Compote

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Berry Compote

  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 orange

Instructions

  1. Whisk dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Mix buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir until just combined.
  4. Heat skillet over medium, lightly grease, pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden.
  6. Simmer berries, sugar, lemon juice, and zest until thickened for compote.
  7. Serve pancakes stacked, topped with compote, maple syrup, and optional mascarpone.
