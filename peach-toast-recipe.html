🍑 Peach Toast

This recipe is a memory I can eat.

🕯️ The Ritual

I made this toast the morning after my grandmother passed. The peaches were overripe, the bread was thick, and the honey was the kind she used to stir into her tea. I didn’t plan it—it just happened. And somehow, it felt like she was there.

This isn’t just breakfast. It’s a ritual of remembrance. A way to start the day with softness and sweetness. A way to say: I’m still here, and so are you.

🧾 Ingredients

  • 2 slices thick sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe peach, sliced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp ricotta or mascarpone
  • Pinch of sea salt

👣 Instructions

  1. Toast the sourdough until golden and crisp.
  2. Spread ricotta or mascarpone generously.
  3. Layer peach slices gently, like placing memories.
  4. Drizzle honey and sprinkle sea salt.
  5. Serve warm. Eat slowly. Remember deeply.
